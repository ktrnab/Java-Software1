Colorado

This homepage is inspired by Colorado. I am currently in Colorado if you can't tell. I love it here. You will love it too! If you're looking for a state to visit, add Colorado to your trip list. Below I list out my 5 reasons why I love Colorado. Because I love mountains, I also made a table of the top 5 highest peaks in Colorado.

5 Reasons to Love Colorado

  1. There are several mountain peaks.
  2. There is a wide array of wild life.
  3. There a couple of national parks.
  4. There are many breeds of dogs.
  5. There is a huge nature-loving community.
